What is Paçoca?

Paçoca is a delightful candy that's bursting with the flavors of Brazil. It's a sweet and nutty treat that's made with ground peanuts and sugar, then shaped into small, bite-sized pieces that are perfect for snacking. With its crumbly texture and rich, earthy taste, Paçoca is like a little piece of the Brazilian countryside in every bite. It's a candy that's both comforting and exciting, with a complexity of flavors that will transport your taste buds to a land of sunshine and joy. Whether you're looking for a quick pick-me-up or a special treat to share with friends, Paçoca is the perfect choice for anyone who loves to indulge in life's sweet pleasures.

Paçoca ingredients

  • Roasted peanuts
  • Sugar
  • Salt
